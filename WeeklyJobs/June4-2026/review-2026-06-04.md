## Notes & flags

1. **3 REJECTs confirmed against JD text:** #3 IDR dead listing (Rule 5); #4 Zillow field position (Rule 4); #13 Red Ventures explicitly hybrid (Rule 4).
2. **workType tag unreliable:** #4 was tagged Remote by the extractor but the JD is a field position. The JD always wins over the tag.
3. **Heuristic jobType is noisy:** Rows 1, 10, 15, 16 scraped as GIG/FREELANCE where CSV labels are more accurate. Do not autocorrect CSV from scrape.
4. **Salary in two wrong directions:** Scrape *invented* data (#5 — LinkedIn sidebar) and *missed* real data (#17 — $54.5k–65k in JD; #6 and #7 — INR ranges). Reconcile salary fields from JD by hand. Never trust a scraped salary without JD confirmation.
5. **Row 8 companyWebsite bug:** CSV has `www.linkedin.com` as the company website for vyom. The correct value is `https://vyom.quest` (from Table A). Fix in CSV before publishing.
6. **Row 7 title truncated in CSV:** CSV shows "Illustrator" but the full title is "Illustrator & Customer Service Associate (Remote)." The truncation obscures the dual-role nature that triggers the Rule 6 REVIEW.
7. **REVIEW rows — required human actions:**
   - #5 Charlotte Party Rentals: verify employer legitimacy and that the role is still open.
   - #7 Gecko Programmes: confirm illustration is the primary function, not CS.
   - #8 vyom: verify employer at `vyom.quest` before publishing; fix companyWebsite in CSV.
   - #9 Ludere Studios: confirm studio is real and application link is active.
   - #11 Calla Press: decide whether to hold (closed) or remove (no stated compensation).
   - #15 Ladders: confirm end-client identity or remove if aggregator-only listing is policy-rejected.
